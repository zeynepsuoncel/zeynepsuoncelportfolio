Marciano by GUESS

Conceived as a sanctuary of refined elegance within the bustling environment of luxury retail, the Marciano by GUESS project investigates the delicate equilibrium between Mediterranean warmth and minimalist restraint. The spatial arrangement is orchestrated with a deliberate rhythm, utilizing soft illumination and high-end material selections to accentuate the quality of the garments while offering the visitor a tactile, serene experience.

"The lounge and checkout zones serve as the fundamental anchors of the space. The contrast established between deep walnut tones, brushed brass detailing, and softly textured stone walls creates a timeless, haptic backdrop."

Secondary Concept

Store-in-Store Design

An independent "store-in-store" exhibition area that seamlessly integrates with the overarching design trajectory while structurally maintaining its own distinct identity. A central minimalist wooden podium serves as a focal point for capsule collections, guiding the visitor's journey.

"The transparency of details and brushed metal finishes advocate for the idea that even temporary capsule exhibition spaces must possess a lasting architectural quality."
