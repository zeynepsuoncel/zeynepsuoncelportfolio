Marciano Retail Concept

Store-in-Store Design

Maintaining an autonomous presence within the wider Marciano ecosystem, this in-store exhibition unit defines a transparent yet bounded zone for the brand's exclusive capsule collections. The central minimalist podium and precision lighting direct the viewer's focus entirely toward the texture and quality of the garments.

"The transparency of details and brushed metal finishes advocate for the idea that even temporary capsule exhibition spaces must possess a lasting architectural quality."
