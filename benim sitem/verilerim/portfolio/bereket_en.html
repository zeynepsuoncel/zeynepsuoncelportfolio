Bereket Panel

Reinterpreting traditional motifs through a contemporary spatial lens, the Bereket Panel elevates surface design from mere cladding into a sculptural focal point. By embedding the organic, fruitful forms of Mediterranean identity within a minimalist structure, it establishes a silent, tactile dialogue between the past and the future.

At the heart of the Bereket Panel lies the ancient symbolic language of Anatolian kilim weaving. The primary motifs used—'Elibelinde' (representing femininity, fertility, and motherhood) and 'Koçboynuzu' (representing masculine power, heroism, and abundance)—combine to symbolize family unity and the perpetual cycle of prosperity.
